# Privacy Policy for Navilux
**Last Updated: May 2026**

Welcome to Navilux. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Navilux collects, uses, stores, and protects your information when you use our mobile application.

By using Navilux, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### a) Location Information
Navilux may collect your device's foreground and background location to provide:
* Nearby places, route planning, and map navigation
* City analysis, weather, AQI, and travel recommendations
* **Destination Alerts:** If enabled, the App tracks your location in the background to calculate remaining distance and notify you when you near your destination. This tracking automatically stops once the alarm is triggered or manually turned off.

### b) Account Information
When you sign in using Firebase Authentication or other login methods, we may collect:
* Name
* Email address
* Profile photo

### c) Device & Usage Information
We may automatically collect:
* Device type
* Operating system version
* App performance and crash reports
* Analytics data

### d) User Preferences & Trip Data
Navilux may store travel preferences, theme settings, saved searches, and app personalization settings. Additionally, any custom Trip Plans you generate are saved to your account. You have full control over this data and can view, manage, or delete your saved trips and account data at any time.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use collected information to:
* Provide map and navigation services
* Generate AI-powered city analysis and travel insights
* Improve app functionality and user experience
* Personalize recommendations and nearby place suggestions
* Enhance security and prevent misuse
* Analyze app performance and crashes

## 3. Third-Party Services
Navilux uses trusted third-party APIs and services to provide functionality.
These services may process limited information according to their own privacy policies:
* Google Firebase
* OpenWeather API
* Gemini API
* Geoapify API
* Ola Maps API
* GNews API
* Pexels API
* Expo Services

Navilux does not sell personal information to third parties.

## 4. WebView Content
Some sections of Navilux may display external websites or services using WebView technology.
Navilux does not control the content, privacy practices, or policies of third-party websites displayed through WebView.
Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of those services separately.

## 5. Data Storage & Security
We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect user data.
However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is completely secure.
We continuously work to improve security and data protection.

## 6. Permissions Used
Navilux may request the following permissions:
* **Location Permission** → For maps, navigation, and nearby places
* **Internet Access** → For APIs and online services
* **Storage/Photos Permission** → For profile image uploads
* **Notification Permission** → For alerts and updates (if enabled)

Permissions are requested only when required.

## 7. Children's Privacy
Navilux is not directed toward children under 13 years of age.
We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 8. User Rights
